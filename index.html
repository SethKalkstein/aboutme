Coding

Throughout my life - even while selling delicious cheeses such as Cheddar, Gouda, and Beaufort - I always used computers. As a realtor I once again used computers, and most of all loved the fleeting moments where I got to use a conditional statement or two in a spreadsheet. When consulting and managing, I would make scheduling spreadsheets that automatically calcualted shifts covered, hours, conversions, and other financial statistics. In Real Estate, I would use string manipulation to apropriately format multiple letters to other realtors to reflect data appropriate to their buyers, or to sort through and calculate new data to base pricing off of. After thinking long and hard about it, I knew that I could not ignore the urge to program any longer, I must immerse myself in code.
